Hot-Dog

Description

Indulge in the iconic delight of a sizzling hot dog, nestled within a soft, fluffy bun. Bursting with flavor, this classic treat features a savory grilled sausage topped with a choice of condiments such as ketchup, mustard, and onions. Take a savory journey with every bite, as the hot dog brings a delicious blend of textures and tastes.

Ingredients

  • Hot dog buns
  • Hot dogs (sausages)
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup
  • Relish (optional)
  • Onions, chopped (optional)
  • Sauerkraut (optional)
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Chili (optional)
  • Jalape√±os (optional)

Steps

  1. Gather hotdog buns, hotdog sausages, and desired toppings.
  2. Preheat grill or stovetop.
  3. Prepare sausages by making diagonal cuts on top.
  4. Cook sausages for 5-7 minutes, turning occasionally.
  5. Optional: Toast buns on the grill or skillet.
  6. Place cooked sausages in buns.
  7. Add desired toppings.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
