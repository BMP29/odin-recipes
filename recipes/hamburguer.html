Hamburguer

Description

Savor the blissful combination of a beef patty, melted cheddar, fresh lettuce, tomatoes, and pickles, all sandwiched between perfectly toasted sesame seed buns. This classic hamburger is a delectable symphony of flavors, guaranteed to satisfy your cravings with every mouthwatering bite.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef or minced beef
  • Hamburger buns (preferably sesame seed buns)
  • Cheddar cheese (or your preferred cheese)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Pickles (sliced or whole)
  • Onion (sliced or diced)
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Salt and pepper (for seasoning the beef patty)
  • Cooking oil or butter (for grilling or frying the patty)
